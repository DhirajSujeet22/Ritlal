About Ritlal

Welcome to Ritlal Ice Cream, where every scoop tells a story of passion and perfection. Founded with a dream to redefine the ice cream experience, Ritlal is dedicated to crafting indulgent, artisanal treats that delight the senses. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create ice cream that is not only delicious but also crafted with love and the highest quality ingredients. From our creamy classic vanilla to exotic, bold flavors, each creation is a testament to our commitment to excellence. At Ritlal, we believe in more than just ice cream; we believe in creating moments of joy. Our small-batch process ensures that every flavor is meticulously perfected, blending traditional techniques with innovative twists. We source the freshest, natural ingredients, embracing local produce and sustainable practices to bring you an experience that’s both delightful and responsible. Whether you're enjoying a cone on a sunny afternoon or sharing a sundae with loved ones, Ritlal Ice Cream is here to make every moment a little sweeter. Join us on our journey of flavor and fun—because life is too short for ordinary ice cream.

Our Vision

"At Ritlal Industry, we envision a tapestry of taste weaving through the vibrant streets of Bihar and reaching every corner of India. Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to delight palates and spread smiles with our handcrafted ice creams, meticulously churned with love and the finest ingredients. From the bustling cities to the serene villages, we aspire to be the quintessential choice, synonymous with quality, innovation, and a commitment to community. With each scoop, we aim not just to satisfy cravings but to create moments of pure joy, forging connections and memories that transcend borders. Join us on this flavorful journey as we scoop our way into hearts across Bihar and beyond, one cart at a time."

our Aim

Why Ritlal?

At Ritlal Ice Cream, we believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert—it's an experience. Our commitment to quality and creativity sets us apart, ensuring every bite is as unforgettable as the last.

Our Flavors

From timeless classics to innovative new creations, our diverse range of flavors caters to every palate. Here are some of our most beloved options:

  • Velvety Vanilla: Smooth and creamy, this classic never goes out of style.
  • Choco Bliss: Rich, dark chocolate that melts in your mouth.
  • Strawberry Dream: Fresh and fruity, with real strawberry pieces.
  • Mango Magic: A tropical escape with every spoonful.
  • Mint Chocolate Chip: A refreshing twist with a satisfying crunch.
